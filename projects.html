Aadhaar Risk Intelligence System (ARIS)

About Aadhaar Risk Intelligence System (ARIS) is a data-driven analytics project aimed at uncovering meaningful patterns, anomalies, and risk indicators in Aadhaar enrolment and update data. Despite widespread Aadhaar adoption, service demand and update behaviour vary significantly across regions. This project focuses on analyzing these variations at district and pincode levels to better understand societal trends and improve service delivery. ARIS leverages the concept of biometric behaviour (bio-age) versus demographic age (DOB) to identify deviations and unusual patterns. By combining statistical analysis with regional insights, the system highlights areas with potential service stress, higher update demand, or irregular activity patterns.
